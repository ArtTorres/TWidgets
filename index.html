Why TWidgets

When we create applications for terminals, It's common that we have to develop our own tools to obtain a consistent look in our applications.

TWidgets aims to provide a set of ready to use widgets, allowing us to focus on the development of our application.

TWidgets also provide us with a development framework to design our own widgets if necessary.

Give it a try and share your experience.
